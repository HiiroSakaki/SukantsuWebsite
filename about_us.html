About us

Sukantsu is made by three french students as part of their final project for the second year at EPITA, a French programming engineer school.
The group as a team is called Charleston and is composed of Thibault "Hiiro Sakaki" Buatois, Nicolas "Reaper" Memeint, and Manuel "Neral" Tertre.
Sukantsu is written in C, using standard library and CSFML library. Code is open-source, available through the download link on the front page.
The Website is made entirely from scratch using html and css.
